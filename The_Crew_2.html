About The Crew 2

In The Crew 2, there are multiple racing disciplines, in which players try to become successful. The game is based in a free roam, scaled down United States. The game includes cars, airplanes, motorcycles, and boats, all of which have different physics, leading to different gameplay. The Crew 2 was developed by Ivory Tower, published by Ubisoft, and released on June 29, 2018 for use on Microsoft Windows, PS4, and Xbox One as a sequel to The Crew, which was released in 2014. Similar to The Crew, The Crew 2 has a huge emphasis on multiplayer.
