Assetto Corsa

Assetto Corsa is Italian for "race setup" and is a racing simulator developed by the Italian game developer Kunos Simulazioni. The game is designed with an emphasis on realistic racing and a lot of customization and moddability. It was released through Steam Early Access on November 8, 2013. It officially left Early Access on December 19, 2014. Assetto Corsa was published by 505 Games for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, and Kunos Simulazoni for PC on June 3, 2015. On January 20, 2016, the release date of April 22, 2016 was revealed. On February 24, 2016, it was announced that the release date was delayed to June 3, 2016. Then, on May 6, 2016, another delay was announced and the new release dates were August 26, 2016 for Europe and August 30, 2016 for North America.
