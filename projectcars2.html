Racing in Project CARS 2

Project CARS 2

Project CARS 2 is the sequel to Project CARS, which was released in May of 2015. Funding for Project CARS 2 began to open on June 23, 2015. Project CARS 2 is a racing simulator that was developed by Slightly Mad Studios and published and distributed by Bandai Namco Entertainmen. It was released to the world on September 22, 2017 for use on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. On February 8, 2017, it was announced that Project CARS 2 was coming late 2017. Slightly Mad Studios revealed that the game would have 182 cars from 38 manufacturers and many car and race car classes. The game also has 46 tracks, containing 121 layouts. The majority of the tracks are laser scanned circuits.
